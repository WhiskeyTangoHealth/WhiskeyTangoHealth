Whiskey Tango Health offers many eating plans to fit your lifestyle and goals

There are many different options of healthy eating to help you meet your goals. If you just want to eat clean and build muscle or lose weight with one of our many options, we are here for you at Whiskey Tango Health with our staff of dietitians and personal trainers.

Clean Eating

Choose unprocessed natural foods for a healthier and happier you. Clean eating is basically steering clear of unprocessed foods with sugar, white flour, and salt. Shop the outer edges of your grocery store.

Keto Eating Plan

Lose fat quickly by eating mainly protein, fat and healthy carbohydrates. This plan switches your bodies fuel from carbohydrates to fat. You keep your carbs below about 50 grams a day and eat more fat and protein to fulfill your caloric needs. This plan works well for people who want quick results, are motivated, and have enough will-power to stay away from simple carbohydrates.

If It Fits Your Macros (IIFYM)

IIFYM is the easiest eating plan because it doesn't eliminate any foods or require you to eat at specific times. You can eat any food as long as it fits into your carbohydrate, protein, fat, and fiber requirement. Your macros are based on your gender, height, weight and activity level.

Intermittent Fasting

Intermittent fasting can be added to any of our plans. The idea of intermittent fasting is to limit your intake of food to eight to twelve hours of the day. This gives you sixteen to twelve of fasting per day. The idea behind this is that you burn more calories during the fasting period because your body has to utilize its reserves at this time.
