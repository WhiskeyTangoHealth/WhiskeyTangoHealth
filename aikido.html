Hoshi Ken Aikido

"There are no secrets to martial arts, merely stingy people with all the answers."

-Senpai Launius

"So does this mean that you are one of those stingy people?" I replied, expecting to hear some highly esoteric explanation for such a bold statement that was made seemingly out of the blue.

"No, not at all!" he replied. "For you see, I am not a stingy person. The answer is- train more. See? I'd never withhold such vital information from you!" He would laugh and then we would drill...incessantly. This is one of many lessons I've been tasked with passing along. -senpai Sam
